Clients

  • Salomon Brothers

    Established Environmental Services Department and then integrated environmental issues into core asset management and credit activities, including all aspects of environmental risk management on real estate portfolio, while also designed and than implemented review process for IPO’s related to corporate governance, environmental and social risk factors.

  • Eco Inn Experiences

    Global hospitality franchise operation consisting of inns and lodges on all six inhabited continents meeting the needs of upscale adventure- and ecologically- oriented leisure travelers.

  • Wallace Global Fund

    Provided direction and management for endowment of over US$ 300 million. Also managed the development of a fully integrated strategic and operating business plan for the first international environmental stock rating business.

  • NAFTA, Environmental Department

    Designed and created a private equity fund focused on lending and investing in ongoing sustainable businesses in Mexico.

  • National Wildlife Federation

    Established program focused on how financial institutions can integrate environmental issues into their core risk management processes.

  • United Nations Environment Programme

    Established the first private sector financial services environmental initiative in collaboration with six leading European banks and UNEP staff in Nairobi prior to The Earth Summit in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Following the summit, sat on the Executive Committee of the ongoing working group based in Geneva.

  • Swiss Bank Corporation (now UBS)

    Did base line research and then helped business managers at the bank establish the first European based “green investment funds” that leveraged sustainability criteria as a way to maximize financial return.

  • University of North Carolina - Kenan-Flagler Business School

    Helped create and founding Board Member of independent 501 (c) 3, The Center for Sustainable Enterprise.

  • Asian Development Bank

    Designed and ran three separate week-long workshops for the Asian Bank Communities on environmental credit risk management.

  • Finance Institute for Global Sustainability

    Internet based 501 (c)3 focused on providing Google-like access to an electronic library of books, magazines, research reports, articles, and other materials to constituents worldwide on how and why integrating sustainability into the decision making process makes bottom line sense for businesses and public policy.
