  • John Ganzi

    Founder and CEO

    John Ganzi is a start-up expert having worked in the energy, finance and environmental areas for 30 years. Currently he is the President of Environment & Finance Enterprise and Treasurer of the North Carolina Sustainable Energy Association. In 2008 he successfully launched as CEO, the Biofuels Center of North Carolina a well funded quasi-governmental agency focused on establishing a multi-billion dollar biofuels industry in NC. Other start operations John has led this decade include a 501(c)3 non-profit, The Finance Institute for Global Sustainability, and Eco Inn Experiences a for-profit.
